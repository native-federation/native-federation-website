# Blog Series

> The Micro Frontends with Modern Angular blog series — in-depth articles covering Native Federation and Micro Frontend architecture patterns.

The comprehensive "Micro Frontends with Modern Angular" article series covering Native Federation and Micro Frontend architecture with Angular.

## Article Series

The comprehensive article series on Angular Architects covers everything you need to know about building Micro Frontends with Angular:

- [The Microfrontend Revolution](https://www.angulararchitects.io/en/aktuelles/the-microfrontend-revolution-part-2-module-federation-with-angular/) — A multi-part article series covering Module Federation concepts, configuration, advanced patterns, and production strategies. Thanks to the shared API, these articles apply equally to Native Federation.

> **Note:** Although these articles were originally written for Module Federation, they fully apply to Native Federation thanks to the identical API. Simply use imports from `@angular-architects/native-federation` instead.
